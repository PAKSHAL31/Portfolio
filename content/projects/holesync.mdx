## Overview

**Holesync Ball Game** is a competitive 3D ball game created using Unity 3D. The game works on the principle of two sprites controlled with the same inputs, offering a unique and challenging gameplay experience. Players navigate through levels with increasing difficulty, including 3v3 and 4v4 grids. The game rewards players with coins upon completing challenges, which can be used to unlock new features and levels.

### Key Features

- **Challenging Levels**: Includes 70 challenging levels with 3v3 and 4v4 grid setups.
- **Dynamic Obstacles**: New obstacles are introduced as the difficulty increases, making it more challenging.
- **Coin Rewards**: Coins are awarded for completing challenges, allowing players to advance to the next levels.
- **Move Strategy**: The game requires precise moves to prevent running out of moves, adding a strategic element.
- **Skill Showcase**: Prove your skills by completing all the levels and achieving high scores.

## Installation Guide

### Prerequisites
- **Android Device**: Requires Android 4.4 (KitKat) or higher.
- **Google Play Store Account**: To download the game from the Play Store.

### Steps to Install

1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your Android device.
2. Search for "**Holesync Ball Game**".
3. Click **Install** to download the game.
4. Once installed, click **Open** to start playing.

### Running the Game

- Progress through 70 levels, overcome obstacles, and use your coins wisely to unlock new challenges.
- Complete all levels to prove your expertise and showcase your skills.
