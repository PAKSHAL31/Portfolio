## Overview

**Shooty Ball Game** is an addictive 3D ball shooting game developed using Unity 3D and C#. The game provides a continuous, action-packed experience where the player shoots balls to stay alive. The objective is to keep playing until the player's health reaches zero, facing increasingly difficult challenges with each passing moment. The game is available on the Google Play Store for Android users.

### Key Features

- **Endless Gameplay**: Play continuously until your health runs out. The challenge is to survive as long as possible.
- **3D Graphics**: High-quality 3D environments powered by Unity for a dynamic and immersive gaming experience.
- **Dynamic Obstacles**: New challenges appear as the game progresses, requiring quick reflexes and accurate shots.
- **Health Mechanism**: The playerâ€™s health decreases as they make mistakes or fail to avoid obstacles, leading to an eventual game over.
- **Google Play Integration**: Achievements and leaderboards integrated with Google Play for tracking progress and competing with friends.

## Installation Guide

### Prerequisites
- **Android Device**: Ensure you have an Android device with version 4.4 (KitKat) or higher.
- **Google Play Store Account**: To download the game from the Play Store.

### Steps to Install

1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your Android device.
2. Search for "**Shooty Ball Game**".
3. Click **Install** to download and install the game.
4. Once installed, click **Open** to start playing.

### Running the Game

- Play the game by shooting balls and avoiding obstacles.
- Track your progress, challenge yourself to survive longer, and aim for higher scores.
- Compete on global leaderboards to see how you compare to others.
